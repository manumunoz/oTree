Instructions Part 2


Stage 1: Group Choice

In Part 2 you will interact with {{ Constants.others }} other players. At the beginning of every round (in Stage 1) each of you will be choose a group: ● or ▲. You will earn points according to the group you choose in Stage 1 and not according to the group you were assigned to (in case they are different). You will have an appearance (◯ or △) and a label (1 to 7). You can change your group and appearance in each round while your label will be changed by the computer. There are 4 stages in each round.

Stage 2: Relations

In stage 2, you can propose relations to other players. A relation with another player is active if both you and the other player propose a relation to each other. Each relation you propose will cost you {{ Constants.link_cost }} points.

Stage 3: Options

In stage 3, you will choose an option: Up or Down. You will earn points depending on the group you chose in Stage 1, the option you choose, and the number of your active relations choosing the same option as you:

If your chosen group is ● and you:

  • choose Up, you get {{ Constants.liked_gain }} points for each active relation choosing Up (including yourself) and 0 points for any active relation choosing Down

  • choose Down, you get {{ Constants.disliked_gain }} points for each active relation choosing Down (including yourself) and 0 points for any active relation choosing Up

If your chosen group is ▲ and you:

  • choose Down, you get {{ Constants.liked_gain }} points for each active relation choosing Down (including yourself) and 0 points for any active relation choosing Up

  • choose Up, you get {{ Constants.disliked_gain }} points for each active relation choosing Up (including yourself) and 0 points for any active relation choosing Down

Stage 4: Results

In stage 4, you will see how many points you get in that round, which are calculated by the gains of coordinating with your active relations minus the costs of your proposed relations. The border of each player's appearance (◯ or △) shows the option that player chose.

Earnings in the experiment

You will participate for 10 rounds in Part 2 and will interact with the same other {{ Constants.others }} players from Part 1. At the end of the experiment the computer will randomly select two rounds, one from Part 1 and one from Part 2. The total number of points you got in the selected rounds will determine your payment.

After the communication stage ends, each participant will choose one of the four possible actions:

by clicking on the corresponding option.

This will be done on a screen similar to that used to send the messages. The main difference is that the action a participant chooses will be displayed as the filling (inner color) of its symbol instead of the border. It is the action you and the other three participants choose, not the messages sent, what will determine your earnings in Part 1.



At the beginning of each round, in the First message screen, you will observe the actions chosen by each player in the previous round (see the first panel on the left in the image above).

In addition, in all stages you will see a summary of the results in your group (see the second panel on the center of the image above). This will be explained in detail in the following screens. For now, it is important to notice that this summary will inform you of the total number times you have chosen the same action as a group (Total coordinations in group), as well as how many times you have, as a group, coordinated on each particular action.

The action you coordinate on as well as the total number of times you coordinate on, as a group, will determine your earnings.

If at least one player chooses a different action from the rest, each will gain o points in that round. How much money you earn is explained below:

1. Your group can earn a total of {{ Constants.total_group_pay | c }} if you coordinate, as a group, in at least {{ Constants.goal_value }} of the {{ Constants.total_number_rounds }} rounds. Coordinating on {{ Constants.no_goal_value }} rounds or less means you do not earn any money.

2. If you coordinate on {{ Constants.goal_value }} rounds or more, you will earn a share of the {{ Constants.total_group_pay | c }} given by the total points of all players in your group (total group points) and your individual point: divide your individual points by the total group points and multiply it by {{ Constants.total_group_pay | c }}.

Ex. 1: if the total group points are 240 and your individual points are 60, you will earn: (60/240)x40 = 10 dollars.
Ex. 2: if the total group points are 100 and your individual points are 35, you will earn: (35/100)x40 = 5.83 dollars.

Summary

You will participate in groups of 4. Each participant can send a message to the others, twice, and then choose an action. You will gain points depending solely on the action chosen. You only gain points if the four participants coordinate on the same action. If you coordinate in less than {{ Constants.goal_value }} rounds as a group you will not earn any money in Part 1. If you coordinate on {{ Constants.goal_value }} rounds or more, your earnings will be a share of the {{ Constants.total_group_pay | c }} assigned to your group. Your share is given by your individual points and the total group points.

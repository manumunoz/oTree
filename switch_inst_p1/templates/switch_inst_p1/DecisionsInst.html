Stage 1: Relations
In the Relations stage you can propose a relation to those players you want to participate with in Stage 2. You can propose a relation to any player no matter their group or appearance. If you propose a relation to a player who also proposes to you, there is an active relation between the two of you. In Stage 2 you will only participate with the players with whom you have an active relation.

You will see your relations in the display (see Figure 2). There is a light arrow leaving from you for each relation you proposed, and a dark arrow heading towards you for each relation proposed to you. If both arrows are present you have an active relation with that other player. If only one of you proposes a relation but the other does not, there is no active relation between the two of you. *Your relations are illustrated with thicker lines and those of others are thinner.


Fig. 2: Relations

Stage 2: Options
In the Option stage you will pick one of two options: Up or Down . You only pick one option for the stage, not one for each relation. You will get points for each of your active relations picking the same option as you. You will not get any points for an active relation picking a different option than you, nor from a player picking the same option as you, if he is not an active connection.

The option picked by each player is illustrated by the color of his appearance (see Figure 3).

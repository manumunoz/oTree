In this phase you can choose whom you want to propose a connection to. You can propose between 0 and {{ Constants.others }} connections. You will pay a cost of {{ Constants.link_cost }} points for each connection you propose.

If you propose a connection to a participant who also proposes to you, this represents an active connection between the two of you. In Phase 2 (Action) you will only interact with those participants with whom you have an active connection.

The image below illustrates the screen you will see in Phase 1. The numbers on the right explain what each element means.



1. The phase and round you are in.

2. The number of participants with internal symbol ◯ or △

3. The label and symbols of each participant. Yours will be larger.

4. Proposals: To propose a connection, select the checkbox next to a participant’s label. Once you checked all the proposals you want to make, click the “Continue” button.

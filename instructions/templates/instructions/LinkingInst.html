In the Connections phase, you will choose which players you want to participate with in the current round. You do this by proposing a connection to each of them. You can propose between 0 and {{ Constants.others }} connections (see below).

You will pay a cost of {{ Constants.link_cost }} points for each connection you propose, as illustrated in the table (you will have a copy of this table in the summary available in each screen).

If you propose a connection to a player who also proposes to you, there will be an active connection between the two of you. In the Action phase, you will only participate with those players with whom you have an active connection.

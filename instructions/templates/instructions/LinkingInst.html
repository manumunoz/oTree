The first decision you make in a round is whom you want to propose a connection to. You can propose between 0 and 10 connections. A connection is active if you propose to a participant who also proposes to you.
- You will pay a cost for every active connection.


You will see a screen as illustrated on the left. The numbers explain what each element means.


#1 and 2. Your player number (between 1 and 11) and your internal and external symbol, which are the same in every round in Part 1. This header will be shown in every phase and in every round.

#3. Which of the 3 phases you are in: Connections

#4. How many players in your group have internal symbol circle or triangle: E.g., 6 circles and 5 triangles

#5. You will be able to click on the number of each player you want to propose a connection to.

#6. and you will be able to see the symbols of each player in your group. To facilitate identification, your border will be thicker and your number larger than the others.

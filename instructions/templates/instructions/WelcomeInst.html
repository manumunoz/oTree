{% endblock %} {% block content %}


For participating in this experiment you will receive a minimum payment of {{ Constants.min_pay }} Euros. Please read these instructions carefully to find out how you can earn additional money.


All interactions take place through the computers. So, please do not talk or communicate with the other players in any other way.


If you have any question please raise your hand and an experimentalist will come and answer your question privately.


This experiment is anonymous. Therefore, your identity will not be revealed to the other players nor theirs to you.



The experiment has 2 parts of 10 rounds each (20 in total). You will first read the instructions for Part 1. Once part 1 is over you will read the instructions for Part 2. In both parts you will interact with the same players.



At the end of the experiment the computer will randomly select two rounds (one from Part 1 and one from Part 2). The total number of points you earned in the two selected rounds will determine your payment. We will convert the points into Euros using the following exchange rate: {{ Constants.exchange }} points = 1 Euro. You will receive your earnings in cash.

For participating in this experiment you will receive a minimum payment of {{ Constants.min_pay }} pesos. Please read carefully these instructions to find out how you can earn additional money.


All interactions take place through the computers. So, please do not talk or communicate with the other participants in any other way.


If you have any question please raise your hand and an experimentalist will come and answer your question privately.


This experiment is anonymous. Therefore, your identity will not be revealed to the other participants nor theirs to you.


In this experiment you can earn points, which will be converted into Colombian pesos at the end of the experiment. We will use the following exchange rate: 50 points = 3000 pesos. You will receive your earnings in cash.



The experiment has 2 parts. You will first read the instructions for Part 1. Once part 1 is over you will read the instructions for Part 2.

Each part lasts 10 rounds (20 in total). The total number of points you get in the 20 rounds will determine your final earnings. In both parts you will participate with the same group.

In the second phase, you will see the connections proposed and active connections formed, and you will choose an action: Up or Down (see below).

The image below illustrates the screen you will see in Phase 2. The numbers explain what each element means.



1. The network of connections formed in Phase 1:

  • A light arrow from you to another player is a connection you proposed
  • A dark arrow to you from another player is a connection proposed to you
  • When both arrows are present you have an active connection with the other player

2. Actions: To choose an action Up or Down click on the corresponding button. You will earn points depending on your group and the decisions made by your active connections, independently of your connections' group. This is as follows:

If your group is ◯ and you:

  • choose Up, you get {{ Constants.liked_gain }} points for each active connection choosing Up
  • choose Down, you get {{ Constants.disliked_gain }} points for each active connection choosing Down
If your group is △ and you:
  • choose Down, you get {{ Constants.liked_gain }} points for each active connection choosing Down
  • choose Up, you get {{ Constants.disliked_gain }} points for each active connection choosing Up

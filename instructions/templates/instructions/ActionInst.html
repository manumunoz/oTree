In this phase you will choose an action: Up or Down.

You can get points for every participant choosing the same action as you (coordinate), but only if there is an active connection between the two of you (you proposed to each other in Phase 1).

The number of points you earn depends on your internal symbol and the action you choose. This is explained below.


You will see a screen as illustrated on the left. The numbers explain what each element means.


#1. You will see which active connections you formed in Phase 1.

- A darker line from you with an arrow pointing to another participant is a connection you proposed

- A lighter line with an arrow pointing towards you is a connection that another participant proposed to you

-An active connection with another participant is formed if both of you proposed to each other

#2. You will have to click on the action you want to choose: Up or Down

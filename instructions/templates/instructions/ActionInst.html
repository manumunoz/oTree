In this phase you will see the active connections you and the other participants formed in Phase 1 (Your connections are darker than the connections of other participants to make it easier for you to see them). You will choose an action: Up or Down. You will earn points for each of your active connections choosing the same action as you (coordinate). The number of points you earn depends on your group and the action you choose. This is explained as follows.

The image below illustrates the screen you will see in Phase 2. The numbers explain what each element means.



1. The network of connections formed in Phase 1:

  • An arrow from you to another participant is a connection you proposed
  • An arrow to you from another participant is a connection proposed to you
  • When both arrows are present there is an active connection with the other participant

2. Actions: To choose an action Up or Down click on the corresponding button. Depending on your symbol and the decisions made by the your active connections you can earn points as follows:

If your group is ◯ and you:

  • choose Up, you get {{ Constants.liked_gain }} points for each active connection choosing Up
  • choose Down, you get {{ Constants.disliked_gain }} points for each active connection choosing Down
If your group is △ and you:
  • choose Down, you get {{ Constants.liked_gain }} points for each active connection choosing Down
  • choose Up, you get {{ Constants.disliked_gain }} points for each active connection choosing Up

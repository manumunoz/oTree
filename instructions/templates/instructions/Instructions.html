Instructions: Summary



Symbols and labels

In this experiment you will interact with {{ Constants.others }} other participants. At the beginning of the experiment each of you will be assigned to one of two groups: circle or triangle, and will be assinged symbols (external and internal) and a label. Your symbols are fixed for all 10 rounds in Part 1 while your label will change in each round.


Phase 1: Connections

In Phase 1, you can propose connections to other participants. Each proposed connection will cost you 2 points. If you propose to a participant who also proposes to you, the connection between the two of you is active.


Phase 2: Action

In Phase 2, you will choose an action: Up or Down. You will earn points for each of your active connections that chooses the same action as you (coordinate), including yourself. The points you earn depend on your group and the action you choose as follows:

If your group is ◯ and you:

  • choose Up, you get {{ Constants.liked_gain }} points for each active connection choosing Up
  • choose Down, you get {{ Constants.disliked_gain }} points for each active connection choosing Down
If your group is △ and you:
  • choose Down, you get {{ Constants.liked_gain }} points for each active connection choosing Down
  • choose Up, you get {{ Constants.disliked_gain }} points for each active connection choosing Up


Phase 3: Results

In Phase 3, you will see a summary of your choices and points. You will see how many of your active connections your coordinated with, how many points you get from your coordinations minus the cost you pay for your proposed connections.



The experiment has 2 parts of 10 rounds each (20 in total). In both parts you will interact with the same other {{ Constants.others }} participants. At the end of the experiment the computer will randomly select one round from Part 1 and one round from Part 2. The total points you earned in the two selected rounds will determine your payment. We will convert the points into Euros using the following exchange rate: {{ Constants.exchange }} points = 1 Euro. You will receive your earnings in cash.

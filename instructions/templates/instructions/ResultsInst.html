In this phase you will see the the actions chosen by your active connections and you will be informed of the points you have in this round for each of your active connections choosing the same action as you.

The image below illustrates the screen you will see in Phase 3. The numbers explain what each element means.



1. The actions chosen in Phase 2, illustrated in the border of each participant's external symbol

  • Purple if action Up
  • Green if action Down

2. Summary of your choices and results:

  • The action you chose
  • Number of active connections
  • Active connections you coordinate with
  • Points you get (points earned minus costs)
  • In case your points (gains minus costs) are negative, you will earn 0 points in that round

These 2 examples illustrate the way points are calculated

Example 1: You are player 3, you are assigned to the group circle, you proposed 4 connections, formed 3 active connections, chose Up and 1 of your active connections chose Up as well (2 chose Down). Therefore, you earn 6 points for coordinating with yourself (you always coordinates with yourself), and 6 (6x1=6) points for choosing the same with 1 other active connection. 12 points in total. From that amount, you subtract the cost of your 4 proposed connections 8 (4x2=8) points. Your earnings in the round are 12-8=4 points in total.


Example 2: You are player 3, you are assigned to the group circle, you proposed 4 connections, formed 3 active connections, chose Down and 2 of your active connections chose Down as well (1 chose Up). Therefore, you earn 4 points for coordinating with yourself (you always coordinates with yourself), and 8 (4x2=8) points for choosing the same with 2 other active connection. 12 points in total. From that amount, you subtract the cost of your 4 proposed connections 8 (4x2=8) points. Your earnings in the round are 12-8=4 points in total.

In the third phase, you will see the the actions chosen by your active connections and the points you get in the current round.

The image below illustrates the screen you will see in Phase 3. The numbers explain what each element means.



1. The border of each player's external symbol shows the action that player chose.

  • Purple if the action is Up
  • Green if the action is Down

2. Summary of the choices you made and the points you get.
To calculate your points count how many of your active connections you coordinate with (including yourself), multiply that number by 4 or 6 (depending on your group and the action you chose), and then subtract the number of connections you proposed multiplied by 2 (the cost of each proposal). This is illustrated in the two examples below.


Example 1: In the illustratin on the right, you are player 3, assigned to the group circle. You chose action Up and coordinated with 1 of your active connections. So you have 2 coordinations (one with yourself and one with player 7), and they are multiplied by 6 (because your group is circle and you chose Up). So you gain 12 points.
You proposed 4 connections (to players 2, 4, 5 and 7), each costs 2 points, so you pay 8 points. In total, you get 12-8= 4 points in this round.


Example 2: In the illustratin on the right, you are player 3, assigned to the group circle. You chose action Down and coordinated with 2 of your active connections. So you have 3 coordinations (one with yourself and one with players 4 and 5), and they are multiplied by 4 (because your group is circle and you chose Down). So you gain 12 points.
You proposed 4 connections (to players 2, 4, 5 and 7), each costs 2 points, so you pay 8 points. In total, you get 12-8= 4 points in this round.

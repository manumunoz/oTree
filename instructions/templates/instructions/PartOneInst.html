At the beginning of the experiment, all participants are randomly assigned to groups of size {{ Constants.names }}. You will be in a group with the same people for all the experiment.

Each participant is assigned a label (1 to {{ Constants.names }}), and an internal and an external symbol (◯ or △), which will not change during the 10 rounds of Part 1.

You will see your label and your symbols on the header at the top of each screen. The example below is the header for Player 1.


Each round in Part 1 consists of 3 phases (see below), which are explained in the following screens.

1. Connections

Participants can propose connections to each other.


2. Action

Each participant observes the connections made and choose an action: Up or Down.

3. Results

Participants observe which of their connections chose the same action, and earn points for it.

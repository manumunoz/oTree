At the beginning of each round, all participants are randomly assigned to groups of size {{ Constants.names }}. You will be in a group with the same people for all the experiment.

Each participant is assigned a label (1 to 11), and an internal and an external symbol. You will be informed of your label and your symbols on the top of the screen (see illustration below), which will not change during the 10 rounds of Part 1.


Each round in Part 1 consists of 3 phases. This is illustrated below and explained in the following screens.

1. Connections

Participants can propose connections to each other.


2. Action

Each participant observes the connections made and choose an action: Up or Down.

3. Results

Participants observe who of their connections chose the same action, and earn points for it.

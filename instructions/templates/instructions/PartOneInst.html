At the beginning of each round, all participants are randomly assigned to groups of size 11. You will be in a group with the same people for all the experiment.

Each round consists of 3 phases, as illustrated below. Each will be explained in the next screens.

1. Connections

Participants can propose connections to each other.


2. Action

Participants observed the connections made and each chooses an action: Up or Down.

3. Results

Participants observe who of their connections chose the same action, and earn points for it.

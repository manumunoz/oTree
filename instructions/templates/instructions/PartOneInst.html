You will participate with {{ Constants.others }} others. At the beginning of the experiment, each participant is randomly assigned to one of two groups: circles or triangles. Participants in each group are assigned an internal and an external symbol (◯ or △) and a label (1 to {{ Constants.names }}). Your group and symbols will not change during the 10 rounds of Part 1, but you will be assigned a different label in every round.

You will see your label and your group (as well as your symbols) on the header of each screen in every round. The header below is an example for a player with label 3 who is assigned to group circle.


Each round in Part 1 consists of 3 phases, which are explained in the following screens

Phase 1. Connections

Participants can propose connections to each other.

Phase 2. Actions

Each participant observes the connections made and choose an action: Up or Down.

Phase 3. Results

Participants observe which of their connections chose the same action, and earn points for it.

Each B-participant will be given information about two A-participants. This information consists of:

  • Each A-participant's chosen name.
  • Number of points scored by each participant in part 1.
Then, the B-participant will choose one of the two A-participants. Every chosen A-participant will do the task again. The counting of points is the same as in part 1. Now, these points are given to the A-participant and the same number of points is given to the B-participant who selected her or him. Note that A-participants who are not chosen will not do a task and will not earn additional points in part 2.

B-participants will not do the task.

Everyone must wait quietly until the chosen A-participants have finished their task.
